# Design Review Executive Sheet

**Purpose:** One-page design assessment tool grounded in cost-displacement and constraint-dynamics vocabulary.

**Posture:** Responsibility-preserving, drift-aware, non-coercive. This is a diagnostic instrument, not a prescription.

---

## Core Questions

### 1. Cost Placement
Where do costs fall? Are they borne by those who benefit, or displaced to others (users, systems, future states)?

### 2. Constraint Visibility
Are operational constraints made explicit, or hidden behind abstraction?

### 3. Drift Detection
How will the system detect when it departs from stated intent?

### 4. Responsibility Preservation
Does the design preserve human authorship and judgment, or substitute structure for decision-making?

### 5. Reversibility
Can decisions be revisited without catastrophic cost? What makes reversal legible?

### 6. Boundary Coherence
Are boundaries defined clearly enough to support critique without requiring doctrinal agreement?

### 7. Propagation Awareness
How do changes propagate? Are downstream effects made visible before commitment?

### 8. Non-Coercive Emergence
Does the design allow for adaptation without forcing premature closure?

---

## Application

This sheet supports early-stage design review. It does not replace judgment, domain expertise, or stakeholder consultation.

Use: Read each question in context of the system under review. Flag gaps, ambiguities, or unexamined assumptions. Document findings. Iterate.
