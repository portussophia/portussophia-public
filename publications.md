## Foundational Publications

### PS-BIOS-001 — The Declared Interface

[![The Declared Interface](https://assets.portussophia.com/img/site/ps-bios-001-cover.jpg)](https://zenodo.org/records/21192767)

*Foundational Declaration*

The governing declaration for PortusSophia™ defines what the programme may claim, what it excludes, how downstream work inherits constraints, and how standing remains visible. It is the proper starting point for evaluating the research as a governed body rather than as a collection of isolated papers.

**Read:** https://zenodo.org/records/21192767

### Series Integrator

[![Series Integrator](https://assets.portussophia.com/img/site/series-integrator-cover.jpg)](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/cf_dev/AbsByAuth.cfm?per_id=9648792)

*Dependency Map and Reader's Guide*

The integrator maps the Philosophy of Science Companion Series as a dependency-bearing sequence. It provides audience-specific reading paths, a cross-paper glossary, and guardrails for understanding how TRI, PLA, FTM, and EPS relate without collapsing them into a single doctrine.

**Read:** https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/cf_dev/AbsByAuth.cfm?per_id=9648792

### A Field Guide to Epistemic Failure

[![A Field Guide to Epistemic Failure](https://assets.portussophia.com/img/site/field-guide-cover.jpg)](https://leanpub.com/afieldguidetoepistemicfailure)

*Diagnostic Guide*

This book names recurring ways knowledge-bearing systems fail even when the needed information is present. Its diagnostic modes show how knowledge can lose function across carrying, classification, context, governance, and cost—giving practitioners vocabulary for breakdowns they may already recognize but cannot yet name.

**Read:** https://leanpub.com/afieldguidetoepistemicfailure

### Contemplation

[![Contemplation](https://assets.portussophia.com/img/site/contemplation-cover.png)](https://leanpub.com/contemplation)

*The Interior Forms of Epistemic Failure · Reflective Companion · Free*

Contemplation turns the carrying problem inward. Through fifteen recognizable patterns, it examines why hard-won understanding may become unavailable precisely when a person needs it, offering recognition and a vocabulary for reflection rather than advice, judgment, or self-help prescriptions.

**Read:** https://leanpub.com/contemplation
